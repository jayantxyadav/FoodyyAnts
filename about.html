Our Story

Discover the passion behind FoodyAnts

From Dream to Reality

Founded in 2020, FoodyAnts began as a small family-run café in the heart of Bhopal. Our founder, Chef Rahul Verma, after spending 15 years working in luxury hotels across Europe and Asia, returned to his hometown with a vision - to create a restaurant that blends international culinary techniques with local flavors.

What started as a 20-seat café has now grown into one of Bhopal's most loved dining destinations. The name "FoodyAnts" represents our philosophy - just like ants work tirelessly and collectively to create something amazing, our team works together to create extraordinary dining experiences.

Five years later, with numerous culinary awards and thousands of happy customers, we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots - serving food made with love, passion, and the freshest ingredients.

Our Journey

Key moments in the FoodyAnts story

2020

FoodyAnts opens its doors in Bhopal with a small 20-seat café.

2021

Expansion to a 60-seat restaurant after receiving the "Best New Restaurant" award from Bhopal Food Critics Circle.

2022

Launch of our signature dish "Tandoori Mushroom Risotto" which becomes an instant hit.

2023

Featured in "India's Top 50 Restaurants" publication and started our community outreach program.

2024

Renovation and interior redesign to create our current beautiful ambiance.

2025

Celebrating our 5th anniversary with a new extended menu and special events throughout the year.
